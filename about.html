About Me

My name is Brillian, a graduate of SMK Negeri 1 Duduksampeyan majoring in Industrial Mechanical Engineering. After graduating, I worked as a machine operator in industry for one year, while continuing my D3 education at the Politeknik Semen Indonesia with an Information Technology study program. My experience is in the industrial world, in particular in machine supervision and operation, has given me deep insight into production efficiency and quality.

Currently, I am focusing on developing myself as a web developer, with expertise in various technologies such as Tailwind CSS, Node.js, and the basics of React.js. I am also active in the Information Technology Student Association organization, where I am involved in building a community to improve student skills and relationships. My latest project is the development of the HELU platform, an English language learning platform for high school/vocational school students with a freemium approach, designed to provide a more interactive and effective learning solution.
